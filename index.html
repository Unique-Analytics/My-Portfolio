Our Mission

We contextualize the who, what, where, why, and how of your target audiences or customer segments. We Analyze hundreds of data points across all of your consumer attributes and actions such as demographics,lifestyle,climate,cultural, household, and propensities for specific brands to gain a 360 degree view of your audiences and how they overlap with one another. Audience Analytics provides you with the tools necessary to develop newer and stronger target audiences, improve content personalization, and increase campaign performance. Knowing these answers can make the difference in the success and failure of any product or service.

Our Data Discovery Process


Designated Marketing Area

(DMA) is a region where the population can receive the same (or similar) television and radio station offerings,and may also include other types of media including newspapers and Internet content. We use DMA strategically as an integral part of our analytic solutions.

Cultural

Culture turns a person into a people, turning the perception of 'I' into 'We'. Different cultural groups think, feel, and act differently. A culture is a way of life of a group of people that are passed along by communication and imitation from one generation to the next. Analyzing the culture of your target audience delivers your business strategy.

Demographics

We use the 4 quadrants methodology in our analysis of Demographics. This essentially says that the more demographics your product or service reaches, the more successful your campaign will be. Our goal is to ensure that your product or service accomplishes this task.

Sociographic

We take the target market down to the level of the individual, where we determine the specific values, attitudes, friends, hobbies, passions, and influences. Essentially, it allows us to discover what really pushes your customers buttons.

Geographics

We fully embrace that home is where your heart is. Using Designated Market areas we discover how a customers environment inflences their buying behavior.

Sentiment Analysis

We take the sentiment of customers seriously, this is why we have developed our own in-house tool that measures the customers sentiment towards a similar brands or product. We don't just tell you they like or dislike a product. We also tell you specifically what they like or dislike about a product/service.
